Chapter 6 Networking

Your internship, interesting part-time jobs and even your career possibilities after biology can all be found through your network.

Your network is simply the people you are in contact with. If you already know people in a field you want to work in, or do an internship, it is so much easier to land a position.

Whether you actively engage in networking or not, everyone has a network. The purpose of today’s lecture by Ilse Huijskens from the Science Career Service is to make you aware of your network, and help you expand it.
